About Liberia Talent Quest.

Liberia Talent Quest is the nation's premier youth talent competition, organised by Legacy Hub Incorporated, Paynesville, Liberia. Open to Liberian citizens and residents between the ages of 15 and 35, LTQ gives performers the platform to be seen, celebrated, and rewarded on a national stage.

Contestants submit a video audition reviewed by a panel of qualified judges. Those who qualify advance through mentorship and into weekly live competition shows, where judge scores and public votes together determine who advances. The season culminates in a grand finale and awards ceremony celebrating Liberia's most outstanding emerging talent.

Competition Details

Contestants submit a video audition for judge review. Approved contestants advance to mentorship and then weekly live competition shows, where the national audience votes for their favorites alongside the judging panel's scores. The season ends with a grand finale and awards.

Rules & Regulations

01

Each contestant may enter only one talent category per season.

02

Performances must be original or properly licensed — no use of copyrighted material without permission.

03

Audition videos must be a single continuous take, no longer than 60 seconds.

04

Decisions made by the judging panel are final.

05

Contestants found to have submitted false information may be disqualified at any stage.

06

Organizers reserve the right to adjust the schedule, format, or rules as needed.

Eligibility Requirements

01

Open to Liberian citizens and residents aged 13 and above.

02

Contestants under 18 must have parental or guardian consent on file.

03

Each applicant must provide a valid phone number and email address.

04

Government-issued ID or birth certificate may be requested to confirm age and identity.

05

Season One winners are not eligible to compete in the same category this season.

These rules and eligibility terms are a starting draft for the organizers to review and finalize before launch.
