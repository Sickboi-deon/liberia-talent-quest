How it works

From audition video to the live stage.

Every contestant goes through the same review process before they appear on the voting page.

Step 1

Submit your audition

Register and upload your audition video directly (MP4, MOV, or WebM, up to 300 MB). Everything is submitted through this site.

Step 2

Judges review

Our judging panel watches every submission and decides who advances.

Step 3

You're confirmed

Selected contestants are confirmed by our team and appear on the public voting page for national voting.

Step 4

Live shows & voting

Weekly live performances, with the national audience voting for their favorites each round.

Step 5

The grand finale

Final performances, winner announcement, and awards.
