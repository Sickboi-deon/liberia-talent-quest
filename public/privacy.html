This Privacy Policy explains how Legacy Hub Incorporated ("Legacy Hub," "we," "our," "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data when you use the Liberia Talent Quest website and participate in the competition.

By registering as a contestant, voting, or otherwise using our platform, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

1. Who We Are

Data Controller: Legacy Hub Incorporated, Paynesville, Liberia.

Contact: legacyhubinc@gmail.com  |  +231 77-874-7441

2.2 Voting data

When you purchase or use a voting code, we record the vote (code used, contestant voted for, timestamp). We do not routinely collect your name or contact details purely for voting, though codes may be linked to a purchase record held by our payment partners.

2.3 Usage data

We automatically collect standard server logs including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps. This data helps us understand traffic patterns and improve the platform.

2.4 Communications data

If you contact us via the contact form, email, or phone, we retain those communications to respond to your enquiry.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Running the competition: Processing registrations, enabling judge scoring, managing performance rounds, and tracking competition results.
  • Communication: Sending you updates about your application status, scheduled events, audition results, and competition news via email and WhatsApp.
  • Promotion: Using your name, stage name, photo, bio, and performance content to promote LTQ on our website, social media, broadcast media, and print materials — with your consent given at registration.
  • Voting system: Validating and tallying public votes, preventing fraud, and publishing leaderboards.
  • Legal compliance: Maintaining records as required by applicable Liberian law.
  • Analytics: Understanding how visitors use our website so we can improve it.

4. Lawful Basis for Processing

  • Contractual necessity: Processing registration data is necessary to manage your participation in the competition.
  • Legitimate interests: Server logs and analytics support website security and improvement.
  • Consent: Use of your photo, video, and likeness for promotional purposes is based on the consent you give by agreeing to these terms at registration. You may withdraw consent for future promotional use by contacting us, though this will not affect material already published.
  • Legal obligation: Certain records may be retained to comply with applicable law.

5. Who We Share Data With

  • Judges: Your name, stage name, county, category, bio, and audition video are shared with LTQ judges for scoring purposes.
  • LTQ staff: Internal staff (Contestant Managers, Finance Managers, Content Managers, etc.) access the data necessary to perform their role.
  • Sponsors (limited): Aggregate, non-identifying statistics (e.g. total participants, county breakdown) may be shared with sponsors. We do not sell individual personal data to sponsors.
  • Service providers: We use third-party providers for email delivery (SMTP), WhatsApp messaging (Meta/WhatsApp Business API), and website hosting. These providers process data only on our behalf and are bound by data protection obligations.
  • Legal authorities: We will disclose data when required to do so by law, court order, or government authority.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for their own marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention

  • Active contestants: Data is retained for the duration of the competition and for three (3) years after the season concludes for record-keeping purposes.
  • Rejected or non-qualifying applications: Registration data is retained for one (1) year, after which it is deleted or anonymised.
  • Voting records: Retained for one (1) year after the season for audit purposes, then deleted.
  • Promotional content (photos, videos, published media): May be retained indefinitely as part of LTQ's historical archive. You may request removal of specific content by contacting us.
  • Server logs: Retained for 90 days.

7. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data, subject to our legal and operational obligations (e.g. active competition participants cannot request deletion mid-season).
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your data, particularly promotional use.
  • Restriction: Ask us to restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time (withdrawal does not affect prior processing).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at legacyhubinc@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies & Browser Storage

We use cookies and similar browser storage technologies to remember your preferences, maintain authenticated sessions where applicable, and improve website functionality. This includes storing your display preference (light or dark mode), your session state if you log in as a staff member or qualified contestant, and minor interface preferences such as dashboard layout. No passwords are stored in browser storage.

We also use Google Analytics, which sets its own cookies to measure website traffic. See Section 9. You can disable analytics cookies via your browser settings or a browser extension such as uBlock Origin.

9. Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics (GA4): We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google may process your IP address and browsing behaviour. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: Fonts are loaded from Google's servers, which may process your IP address as a standard server request.
  • Email delivery provider (SMTP): We use a configured SMTP provider to send notification emails. Your name and email address are transmitted to this provider solely to deliver your message.
  • WhatsApp Business API (Meta): If WhatsApp notifications are enabled, your phone number and notification content are transmitted via Meta's WhatsApp Business API. See WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.
  • Payment providers (Orange Money, MTN Mobile Money): Registration fees are processed through these third-party mobile money services. Legacy Hub does not receive or store your mobile money PIN or full account credentials. Each provider operates under its own privacy policy and terms of service, which govern the payment transaction independently of Legacy Hub.

10. Children's Privacy

Contestants between the ages of 15 and 17 may participate with the written consent of a parent or legal guardian. Legacy Hub reserves the right to request proof of age and parental or guardian consent at any stage of the competition. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has submitted data without your consent, please contact us immediately and we will remove it.

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 15.

11. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures, including secure password storage using industry-standard hashing algorithms, access controls, session management, audit logging, and encryption where appropriate, to protect personal information against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

Access to personal data is restricted to staff members who require it to carry out their specific role. Significant staff actions are logged for accountability purposes.

No system is completely secure. If you have reason to believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately at legacyhubinc@gmail.com.

If we become aware of a security incident affecting your personal information, we will take appropriate steps consistent with applicable law, including notifying affected individuals where required.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. We will notify registered contestants of material changes via email where practicable.
