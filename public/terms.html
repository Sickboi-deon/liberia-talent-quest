1. Eligibility

  • Contestants must be Liberian citizens or legal residents of Liberia.
  • Contestants must be between 15 and 35 years of age at the time of registration. Participants under 18 must provide written consent from a parent or legal guardian.
  • Contestants must reside in or be able to travel to Paynesville, Liberia for live performance rounds.
  • Employees, directors, judges, and immediate family members of Legacy Hub Incorporated are not eligible to participate as contestants.
  • Previous winners of LTQ Season One are not eligible to compete as contestants in Season Two.
  • Eligibility to compete in live rounds is subject to successful completion of registration, payment of the registration fee, and passing the audition review stage.

2. Registration & Fees

  • Registration is completed online via the official LTQ website. Each person may submit only one application per season.
  • A non-refundable registration fee is required to confirm your application. The amount and accepted payment methods (Orange Money, MTN Mobile Money) will be communicated via the confirmation email sent after initial submission.

4. Judging & Qualification

  • Audition videos are reviewed by a panel of qualified judges appointed by Legacy Hub. Judging criteria include stage presence, technical skill, originality, and audience appeal.
  • Qualification decisions are based on aggregate judge scores against pre-set thresholds. These thresholds are set by Legacy Hub and are not publicly disclosed.
  • Contestants who score above the qualification threshold are invited to the live competition. Contestants who score in the waitlist band may be invited if qualified spots become available.
  • All judging decisions are final. No correspondence will be entered into regarding individual scores or outcomes.

5. Live Performance Rounds

  • Qualified contestants must be available on the scheduled performance dates communicated by the Contestant Manager. Failure to perform without prior authorised excuse constitutes forfeiture.
  • Contestants are responsible for their own transport to and from the venue unless otherwise arranged by Legacy Hub.
  • Performance order, slot lengths, and stage configuration are determined by the organising team and may change at short notice.
  • Each live round eliminates contestants based on a combined score of judge marks and public votes. The weighting of each component is set by Legacy Hub.
  • The final round produces one overall winner.

6. Voting

  • Public voting is open during designated competition rounds only. Voting outside these windows has no effect.
  • Each voting code is single-use and tied to one vote for one contestant.
  • Voting codes are purchased through authorised channels. Obtaining or using codes through unauthorised means may result in votes being voided.
  • Automated voting, bulk purchasing of codes for fraudulent purposes, and any attempt to manipulate vote counts is strictly prohibited. Such activity may be reported to the appropriate authorities where unlawful conduct is suspected.
  • Registering under a false identity, submitting fraudulent applications, impersonating another person, or providing forged parental or guardian consent is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate disqualification and may be referred to the appropriate authorities.
  • Legacy Hub reserves the right to invalidate suspicious votes at its sole discretion.
  • Votes are a contributing factor in competition outcomes but are weighted alongside judge scores. The exact weighting is set by Legacy Hub.
